## Style Prompt

Xiaohongshu-style AI tool tutorial with a witty, creator-friendly voice. The video should feel like a polished saved-note card brought to life: clean vertical layout, high readability, compact information blocks, and playful micro-interactions. Use a dark ink canvas with warm off-white text, coral-red Xiaohongshu accents, mint highlights, and amber notes. Keep the energy tutorial-first, humorous, and practical.

## Colors

- Canvas: `#17171B`
- Surface: `#24242B`
- Text primary: `#FFF7EA`
- Text secondary: `#D8CFBE`
- Xiaohongshu coral accent: `#FF4F61`
- Mint highlight: `#56E0B8`
- Amber note: `#FFD166`

## Typography

- Headlines: `Arial Black`, `PingFang SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif
- Body and captions: `PingFang SC`, `Microsoft YaHei`, sans-serif
- Code and prompt text: `IBM Plex Mono`, `SFMono-Regular`, monospace

## Motion

- Snappy tutorial-card entrances with short overshoot.
- Primary transition: vertical push / note-card slide, 0.35-0.5s.
- Accent transition: quick sticker pop and scan-line wipe for topic changes.
- Keep text large and readable for mobile viewing.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not make it look like a corporate SaaS landing page.
- Do not use tiny web UI text that cannot be read on mobile.
- Do not rely on random AI-video aesthetics or abstract footage.
- Do not use jump cuts between scenes.
- Do not let humor obscure the actual workflow.
