## 最终生成提示词

Use the loaded images only as identity references for the same adult East Asian woman. Preserve her recognizable oval face, softly defined jawline, calm almond-shaped dark eyes with restrained cool gaze, natural straight brows, small straight nose, relaxed natural lips, clear natural skin texture, adult age impression, slender natural adult proportions, and short dark bob hairstyle with light bangs. Do not copy the reference clothing, coffee truck, indoor room, window scene, or poses. Change the scene, clothing, pose, lighting, camera, and design style according to this prompt. Do not alter her facial identity.

Create one ultra-detailed photorealistic square image, aspect ratio 1:1. The composition is an ultra-modern luxury smartphone gallery interface, like a premium fashion social media campaign screen. Multiple floating image cards are stacked and overlapping with subtle depth, soft shadows, glassy UI layers, soft blur transitions, and clean modern spacing. Each card shows the same female fashion model in a different editorial pose, all clearly the same person with consistent short dark bob and face identity.

Central card: enlarged, vertical fashion editorial portrait card, glowing softly with warm sunset tones and golden rim light. She wears an elegant cream blazer, silk top, and statement sunglasses; pose is confident but understated, premium fashion campaign mood. Face should remain visible enough to judge identity; sunglasses should be slightly lowered or semi-transparent enough that her eye shape and gaze are partly readable.

Surrounding cards: smaller floating gallery cards showing alternate poses of the same woman in the same outfit styling: one three-quarter side pose adjusting sunglasses, one close-up of blazer lapel and silk texture with her lower face, one walking pose with blazer movement, one cropped portrait with sunset light on hair. Keep all cards inside the square frame with a sophisticated depth hierarchy.

UI design details: minimal smartphone-gallery interface elements such as tiny timestamps, small generic captions, thin progress indicators, understated rounded card corners, subtle status-bar-like marks, minimal icons, and premium editorial metadata. Use only generic UI microcopy such as “Today”, “18:42”, “Golden Hour”, “Editorial”, “5 Photos”, “Select”, or abstract short labels. Do NOT include any real city names, country names, brand names, logos, watermarks, or location labels. Text should be minimal and realistic, not large slogans; avoid unreadable clutter.

Overall design language: luxury social-media aesthetic, modern, polished, cream, warm gold, soft charcoal accents, subtle glass reflections, clean depth, premium fashion campaign style. Warm sunset tones on the central card, soft studio/editorial realism, high-end fashion photography, crisp fabric texture on cream blazer and silk top, refined natural skin texture with minimal retouching, natural anatomy, believable hands, high-quality finished image.

Avoid face drift, different person, childlike appearance, long hair, different hair color, heavy plastic skin, excessive makeup, malformed hands, extra fingers, broken anatomy, watermark, logo, real place names, distorted UI, large messy text, clutter covering the face, or a single flat collage without depth.
